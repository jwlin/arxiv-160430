New Account

Instructions

If you have already registered a GradApp account and you do not remember your password, please use our account retrieval tool.

If you are already a UA student, faculty, or staff member and have UA NetID, please skip this step and instead login with your NetID.

If you are registering a new account:

  • IMPORTANT: You will need your own email address, which will be your username and point of contact for this Graduate Admissions Application system.
  • After clicking Create account below, you will receive an email with instructions on how to start the application process.

Please choose a strong password to help protect your personal information. The following are required:

  • A minimum of 11 characters
  • At least one lower-case letter
  • At least one upper-case letter
  • At least one number
  • At least one special character: ! @ # $ % ^ & * ( ) - + =

Important: All applicants must have unique and individual email address to use this system. If you are an Agency applying for multiple applicants, please be sure to create individual accounts using unique email addresses for each applicant.
