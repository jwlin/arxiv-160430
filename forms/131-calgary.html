What is an eID?
 Your eID will be your personalized unique ID for electronic access here at the UofC.
What should I choose as my eID?
Your eID must end with .lastname. The eID we recommend is firstname.lastname (e.g. Joe.Smith).
My eID was already taken...
You can pick any username that ends with .lastname. For example, try a nickname.lastname
What is my UCID?
Your UCID is the number on the front of your UofC ID card. Please enter only the digits.
Add another UCID?
Some users may have more than one UCID.  You can add as many as you own by clicking Add another UCID.
What is my display name?
The myUofC portal will address you by this name. We suggest you use simply your first name.
