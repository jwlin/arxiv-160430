Thank you for your interest in studying at NCTU.

Log in using your existing e-mail address and set a password to accessing this online application system. Each applicant may create ONE account only. Applicants with multiple accounts will be disqualified from application. The system will send a verification notice to your email account. Please check your email in order to complete the process.

Please make sure the email address you provided is active and check your email regularly as important message will be sent via email.

Your password can be 6-12 characters a-z or 0-9 and cannot contain any spaces.
