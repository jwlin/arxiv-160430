Welcome to The Graduate School’s online application.

The Graduate School offers the following three features via this online tool: submit your application, view your decision, and submit your enrollment decision. For your convenience, you will use this same website, pin and password to access these three features. If at anytime you forget your pin or password, you may use the "Forgot your PIN or password?" feature in the User Login box to obtain your information.

Please be sure to check the status of your admission decision via this interface. Once an admission decision has been rendered, a link to view your decision will appear on the online application "My Applications" page. If no link is present, an admission decision has not yet been rendered.

Applying:
This application should be completed by all students who wish to enroll in The Graduate School as either degree seeking or non-degree seeking students. Please be sure to review The Graduate School’s admission website before starting your online application. Because application fees cannot be returned it is very important that you verify that your program is offered through The Graduate School before you start your application. A list of Northwestern University’s academic programs is available here. 

Please be sure to turn off your pop up blocker at this time. Important messages may be communicated to you through Pop Up Windows during the application process.

System Requirements

Application System Requirements

Supported browsers include Internet Explorer 9 and 11, Firefox, Chrome and Safari.

While the majority of features will work with other browsers, we can only guarantee full compatibility and offer support for the browsers mentioned above.

If you are using one of the supported browsers and are experiencing problems, we encourage you to upgrade to the latest version. If you still experience problems after upgrading, please make sure that your browser is set to accept cookies. Also, make sure the following browser settings are enabled:

  • JavaScript must be enabled.
  • Popup blockers must be disabled.

If your browser has a section for trusted sites, please enter these two URLs:

  • http://app.applyyourself.com
  • https://app.applyyourself.com

You will need Adobe Reader 6.0 or higher to download, view and print PDF files. While PDF files should work with most other PDF viewers, we can only guarantee full compatibility and support for Adobe Reader 6.0 or higher.
